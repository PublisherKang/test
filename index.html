  • A number that shows how passionately you are mining Toc, and it represents the amount of mining per second.

    You can toggle the window on/off via the crown icon at the top.

Item Trading

Item Trading

When requesting a transaction from the other party, it is converted to a one-to-one transaction screen, allowing for a secret conversation, and both non-minted equipment and minted NFT equipment can be traded. (Item exchange x)

All are traded with Gem Stone, and in the case of NFT equipment, it is linked in your wallet after item transaction and NFT is automatically traded, so please trade with confidence.

Shop

Shop

In the shop, you can use the booster for a week by purchasing the gold and silver package, you can auto-click by purchasing the items individually, and you can purchase a mining tool to synthesize equipment to level up.

No cost at all!! You can save it with the Jam Stones you have collected.

Item Combine

Item Combine

You can level up by synthesizing equipment obtained through equipment draws and user-to-user transactions.

You can level up by synthesizing 3 pieces of equipment of the same grade and level.

It is possible to combine game items and NFT items, so please be careful when selecting materials!

Make NFT

Make NFT

Game users can mint their equipment with NFT and sell them in the market.

Only equipment of Rare class or higher can be minted.

The generated NFT equipment can be sold for cryptocurrency on the marketplace.
