TERATTO is...

TERATTO is a digital healthcare infrastructure metaverse to be produced through the technology and services of BIOLABS, a platform company that provides B2B services for medical tourism, health checkups, plastic surgery, beauty, and dentistry, and MEBION, which provides B2C-based services.

MEBILABS was established to go global through new business businesses, Metaverse and NFT, due to a career in connecting 200 existing hospitals and providing services to companies and individuals.

Currently, we are doing our best to develop TERATTO, and we will continue to meet and communicate with various users through games and the issuance of various NFTs before TERATTO opens.

TERATTO plans to sugest various medical information and low prices to the people of the world through the world's only digital healthcare metaverse, and will present a new world where hospitals, health and beauty-related companies can easily meet customers and conduct marketing.
